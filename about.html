My Story

I've always aspired to make a meaningful impact in the world through a specific set of skills I’ve committed myself to perfecting. During my school years, I immersed myself in the music industry, ambitiously building my own musical empire. Despite recording and co-producing songs, creating a music video, and publishing an album, I realized the industry's limitations. Recognizing the need for a more sustainable career, I transitioned to the finance and insurance sector at my father's dealership. Initially joining the sales team, I worked diligently to meet the qualifications for becoming a 'fit and proper' financial advisor. Later, when my father expanded to open an aftersales department, I spearheaded its establishment from the ground up. Serving as Aftersales Manager for approximately five years, I aimed to transform the workshop and parts department into the premier service provider in Gauteng. However, challenges emerged as the dynamics of working with family proved complex. Dissatisfied with a stagnant role and seeking professional growth, I discovered my passion for programming as an avenue for building my own future.

My passion for programming

I love that every program has its own set of challenges that need to be solved like a puzzle, and I absolutely love knowing that if something in the code doesn’t work, it’s on me – I control my own work. I’m in love with the idea that the possibilities in changing the world with my code are endless; there are so many opportunities to make a difference in the world through programming. It’s a whole new world for me, and it’s exciting!

My future goals

I’m currently working towards a Bachelor of Science in IT (Software Engineering), and my main interest is backend programming; however, I do have extensive knowledge on front-end development as well. Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning has sparked an interest within me, and in future, I hope to specialize in a field where AI software can work hand-in-hand with the mechanical engineering aspects of technology.
